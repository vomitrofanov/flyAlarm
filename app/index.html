Decision support only — not a safety authority. Scores come from raw model data (Open-Meteo best-match) with simplified site rules; 10 m model wind underestimates launch wind on ridges. Always check the sky, the windsock and local pilots before flying. Sites come from the online ParaglidingEarth database merged with a built-in Bulgarian pack; entries marked “approx. data” need local verification. On each card the compass shows the takeoff sector (solid shading = good direction, faint = possible) and the forecast wind arrow; scores now reward wind blowing straight into the launch axis. Thresholds live in the TIERS block at the top of this file.
